HISTORY

Dhodz Pizza Boljoon, Cebu was founded and established on June 5, 2024, and officially opened on the same date as part of the growing Dhodz Pizza brand, continuing its mission to offer budget-friendly and satisfying pizzas to local communities.

Life of the Owner

Romanito J. Ambrad Sr. is the owner of Dhodz Pizza Boljoon, Cebu, located in Poblacion, Boljoon, Cebu. He is a hardworking man who started the business to support his family and serve the local community. He believes in hard work and honesty. His family is his main inspiration, and this motivates him to do his best every day in managing the pizza shop. Running Dhodz Pizza Boljoon was not easy at first, but he stayed patient and determined. Through effort and dedication, he was able to keep the business running and growing. Today, Romanito J. Ambrad Sr. is known as a responsible business owner and a loving family man who provides affordable and delicious pizza to the people of Boljoon.

Succes of the Business

Dhodz Pizza Boljoon, Cebu quickly became successful after opening in June 2024. Even in a short time, the business gained many customers because of its affordable prices, good taste, and generous toppings. With the hard work of the owner, Romanito J. Ambrad Sr., and good customer service, the pizza shop earned the trust of the local community. Families, students, and groups started choosing Dhodz Pizza as their go-to pizza place.

Challenges of the Owner

Romanito J. Ambrad Sr. experienced several challenges in managing Dhodz Pizza Boljoon, Cebu. At the beginning, he faced limited budget and capital, which made it difficult to cover supplies and daily expenses. Since the business was new, attracting customers and building trust in the community was also a challenge. He worked long hours to make sure the shop operated well, which was physically tiring. Balancing his business responsibilities and family time was another difficulty. Despite these challenges, his patience, hard work, and determination helped him keep the business running and growing.

How will you see your business 10 years after

Ten years from now, I see my Dhodz Pizza Boljoon, Cebu as a stable and well-known local business in our community. I hope it will continue to grow with more loyal customers and strong support from the people of Boljoon. I see the business improving in quality, service, and management, while still keeping prices affordable. I also hope it can create more jobs and help support other families. Most importantly, I see the business remaining family-oriented, trusted, and successful, built on hard work, honesty, and dedication.
